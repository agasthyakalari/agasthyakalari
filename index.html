About Our Centre

Agasthya Bharatha Kalari Sangham Siddha Marma Treatment Centre


We welcome to Agasthya Bharatha Kalari, where we preserve and practice the ancient traditions of Kalaripayattu martial arts and Siddha medicine. Our center is dedicated to holistic healing through time-tested techniques passed down through generations.

Our mission is to provide authentic training in Kalaripayattu and effective Siddha treatments, helping our students and patients achieve physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.

Kalaripayattu and Siddha Marma Gurukkal

With over 35 years of experience, Mohan Kumar Gurukkal is a revered master of Kalaripayattu. He has dedicated his life to preserving and teaching this ancient martial art form, training students from around the world in authentic Kerala traditions.
